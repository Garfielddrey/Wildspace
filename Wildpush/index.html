Our Timeline

Key milestones in wildlife conservation

2005: Establishment

Wildvoice was founded with a mission to protect endangered species through direct action and education.

2010: Amazon Initiative

Launched our first major conservation project in the Amazon rainforest, protecting over 100,000 acres.

2015: Anti-Poaching Task Force

Created specialized teams to combat poaching in African wildlife reserves.

2020: Coral Restoration

Began marine conservation efforts with coral reef restoration projects in the Pacific.

2023: Global Expansion

Expanded operations to 15 countries with over 50 active conservation projects.
